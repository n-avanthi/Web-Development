Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the India national team who plays as a right-handed batsman for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL and for the Delhi in Indian domestic cricket. Born and raised in New Delhi, Kohli trained at the West Delhi Cricket Academy and started his youth career with the Delhi Under-15 team.
He made his international debut in 2008 and quickly became a key player in the ODI team and later made his Test debut in 2011.
